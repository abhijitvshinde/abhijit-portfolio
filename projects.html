CNN-Based Microwave Imaging for Intracranial Hemorrhage Detection

Developed a deep-learning-driven microwave imaging system for non-invasive brain hemorrhage detection using Vivaldi antenna arrays, S-parameter datasets, HFSS/XFdtd simulations, and CNN-based classification pipelines.

Key Contributions

  • Designed and optimized Vivaldi antenna arrays for microwave imaging.
  • Generated and processed S-parameter datasets for hemorrhage localization.
  • Built CNN pipelines for imaging and classification.
